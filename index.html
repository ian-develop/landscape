2026
Physical AI Infrastructure
My investment thesis, 'Physical AI Infrastructure' focuses on the foundational layers required to deploy AI in the physical world. As robots, autonomous systems, and embodied AI move from lab to production, the critical bottleneck shifts from algorithms to infrastructure — simulation & world models, middleware & OS, edge compute, system testing & V&V, and cybersecurity for physical AI. I invest 2–3 years ahead of mainstream adoption into the infrastructure startups that will become deployment prerequisites, at the intersection of software and hardware in regulated enterprise markets.
2024
AI-Driven Deep Tech Transformation
My investment thesis centers on the transformation of deep tech sectors including manufacturing, energy, semiconductors, and space, where traditional software is increasingly replaced or enhanced by AI-driven solutions. Tasks currently dependent on human labor and expertise, as well as processes relying on conventional software systems, are rapidly transitioning to AI-powered alternatives.
2022
Redefining Humanity
My investment thesis, 'Redefining Humanity' centered on the transformative potential of AI, robotics, semiconductors, and advanced technologies — innovations poised to revolutionize industries and liberate humanity from the constraints of labor, paving the way for a future defined by creativity and higher purpose.
